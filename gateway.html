Choosing a Payment Gateway in KSA

An Interactive Guide for React Native Developers (2025)

The Right Choice Matters

Selecting a payment gateway in Saudi Arabia involves more than just transaction fees. It's crucial to support local payment methods like Mada, which dominates the market, alongside international cards and growing digital wallets like Apple Pay and STC Pay. This guide focuses on leading gateways that provide robust support and developer-friendly integrations for React Native applications, helping you make an informed decision based on your business needs.

Our Recommendations

For Startups & Developers: Moyasar

If your priority is a fast, developer-first integration with transparent, competitive pricing and an official React Native SDK, Moyasar is an excellent choice. Their focus on simplicity and strong documentation reduces development time significantly.

For Growing Businesses: PayTabs

If you need a scalable solution with a wide array of features, including invoicing and social media payments, PayTabs is a strong contender. Their tiered plans can accommodate your business as it grows, though you'll want to review the specific fees for your expected volume.

For Large Enterprises: HyperPay

For large-scale operations requiring robust security, extensive customization, and dedicated support, HyperPay is a market leader. Their solutions are trusted by major brands in the region and can handle high transaction volumes with advanced fraud protection.

For Pan-GCC Ambitions: Tap

If your business model involves expansion across the GCC and MENA regions, Tap's unified platform, which supports a vast range of local payment methods from different countries, can simplify your payment infrastructure significantly.
